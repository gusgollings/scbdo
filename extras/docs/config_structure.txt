SCBdo Meet File Structure:

site-config		Site specific configs
meetdir/
	config		Meet configuration
	riders.csv	List of riders and their numbers
	events.csv	List of event numbers, names and types
	results.csv	Meet results in comma sep spreadsheet
	log		Log of all program and timing events
	event_X		Config, starters and results for event XX
	event_X_heat_Y	Config, starters and results for heat YY of event XX
